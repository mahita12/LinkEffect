How it Works

LinkEffect is an innovative tool that leverages your LinkedIn connections via a CSV file, providing targeted search results based on your input. Whether you’re seeking a mentor or a new job opportunity, LinkEffect allows you to specify your desired position or target company—or both. The tool then scans your network and delivers relevant results, complete with a pre-drafted message for you to send to your chosen contact.

Networking

LinkEffect leverages your LinkedIn network to help you find mentors, job opportunities, events, and programs to participate in.

Provides a Custom Message

Not sure what to say to your potential mentor, coworker, or manager? No worries—LinkEffect generates a custom message based on your search criteria. Copy and paste the message to send directly to your connection via LinkedIn, and feel free to customize it as needed.
